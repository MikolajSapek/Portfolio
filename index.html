A Photo Anthology:

Mikołaj Paweł Sapek Portfolio

A collection of photographs captured during travels around the world, all shot on analog film, documenting encounters with people and places across different continents.

Each album tells a story of a journey, moments of connection, landscapes discovered, and the quiet beauty found in everyday life in distant corners of the globe.

Through these images, I seek to preserve the fleeting poetry of human encounters and the unique character of each place visited.

In 2021, I pulled my grandfather's old Zenit camera from the closet. The effects, you could say, are below.
